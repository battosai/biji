Repetitive tasks need to have an equally valuable reward to be engaging. Making a repetitive task required for participation reduces it to a [[202512262237-a|chore]].

Similarly, using something repeatedly does not imbue it with [[202512241316|value]]. The usage itself also needs to have value beyond being a requirement.

Example: Durability in video games ([[Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild|LotZ: BotW]])
